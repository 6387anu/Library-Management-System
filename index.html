Some Famous Book

                
                Engineering and the Mind's Eye

                In this insightful and incisive essay, Eugene Ferguson demonstrates 
                that good engineering is as much a matter of intuition and nonverbal 
                thinking as of equations and computation. He argues that a system of 
                engineering education that ignores nonverbal thinking will produce 
                engineers who are dangerously ignorant of the many ways in which the real 
                world differs from the mathematical models constructed in academic minds

                by Eugene Ferguson is Professor of History Emeritus 
                   at the University of Delaware               
            
                    
 The Perfectionists:How Precision Engineers 
     Created the Modern World


        The revered New York Times bestselling author traces the development of 
        technology from the Industrial Age to the Digital Age to explore the single 
        component crucial to advancement—precision—in a superb history that is both 
        an homage and a warning for our future

            by Simon Winchester
                
                    
            The Honest Always Stand Alone                    
                    

        The Honest Always Stand Alone is a memoir written by C.G. Somiah, a former 
        Indian Administrative Service (IAS) officer and the eighth Comptroller and 
        Auditor General (CAG) of India. The book chronicles Somiah’s journey as a 
        civil servant, spanning his early days as an Assistant Collector in Orissa 
        to his tenure as Home Secretary and Central Vigilance Commissioner

            by C.G. Somiah

About Our Library

"As a regular user of the library, I can confidently say that our library management system has transformed the way we access and interact with books. 
The online platform is incredibly user-friendly, making it easy for students and staff alike to find and borrow books with just a few clicks

The extensive catalog covers a wide range of genres and categories, ensuring that everyone can find something that piques their interest. 
The ability to manage rentals and track due dates has made the borrowing process seamless and efficient

Moreover, the feedback mechanism allows us to voice our opinions, and I’ve seen several improvements based on user suggestions. 
The staff is responsive and dedicated, always willing to assist when needed

Overall, our library isn’t just a place to borrow books; it has become a vibrant community hub, thanks to the thoughtful integration of technology. 
I wholeheartedly recommend our library management system to anyone looking to enhance their reading experience!"

                                                                                                                                                                 Library Director
